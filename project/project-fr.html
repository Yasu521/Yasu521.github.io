Doshisha Global Business and Management Studies

2021 - 2023 #Global Business Management #Coaching

While studying management knowledge and organizational operations in graduate school, I conducted research on organizational transformation in the Japan Self-Defense Forces.
Additionally, with the goal of fostering mutual learning during and after my studies, I established a coaching community where members could practice coaching together. Under the guidance of experts, I gathered over 180 members and engaged in activities for two years.
As part of my activities, I conducted a survey on career paths among third-year undergraduate students and found that over 70% of them were unable to envision their career plans. Feeling a sense of urgency about this situation, I thought about creating opportunities not only for students but also for professionals to gain new perspectives. To this end, I organized an event where we invited corporate executives to engage in dialogues with students.
