1. Use of Cookies by Third-Party Advertising Providers

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on users' prior visits to the Site or other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables Google and its partners to serve ads to users based on their visit to the Site and/or other sites on the internet.

2. Personalization and User Control

Users may opt-out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Alternatively, users can visit www.aboutads.info to opt-out of the use of cookies for personalized advertising by third-party vendors.

3. Third-Party Advertising Networks

If third-party advertising networks or vendors are not opted out of delivering ads, their cookies may be used during ad serving. We explicitly provide the following details for transparency:

  • Ads served by third-party vendors or ad networks may appear on the Site.
  • Links to the relevant third-party vendors and ad networks are provided below, where users can learn how to disable cookies used for personalized advertising, if supported by the vendor:
    • Google
    • Network Advertising Initiative (NAI)
  • Users can also visit www.aboutads.info for general instructions on how to opt out of the use of cookies for personalized advertising by third-party vendors.

4. Managing Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to enhance user experience and serve relevant advertisements. Users can manage their cookie preferences by modifying their browser settings to disable or delete cookies. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the Site.

5. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. Any updates will be posted on this page, and users are encouraged to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

クッキーは、ユーザー体験を向上させ、適切な広告を表示するために、ユーザーのデバイスに保存される小さなテキストファイルです。ユーザーは、ブラウザの設定を変更することで、クッキーの無効化や削除を行うことができます。ただし、クッキーを無効化すると、本サイトの機能に影響を与える場合があります。
